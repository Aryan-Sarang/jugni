About Us

In a world where women continue to break barriers, shatter stereotypes and inspire changes, their strength, independence and unwavering values shine through. They are the embodiment of modern feminity- strong, independent, bold, yet gracefully feminine. This collection, JUGNI, seeks to celebrate and pay tribute to these remarkable women who effortlessly balance their responsibilities and value everyone around them.

"JUGNI" is a collection that captures the essence of these modern women, showcasing their perfect blend of elegance and boldness.

In the world of fashion, creativity knows no bounds. Its a realm where one's dream can be transformed into exquisite pieces of art. Our journey is not just a tale of entrepreneurial success but also a beutiful way of thanking my mother for her guiding light and views. Lets dwell into the story of how i turned my childhood passion into an Indian wear brand.

The seed of this brand was sown in when I realised that there was a gap in the Indianwear market. While Indian fashion is known for its opulence and grandeur, it often falls short in terms of comfort and functionality. I envisioned a brand that was harmoniously blend the traditional aesthetics of Indian wear with comfort, functionality, and meticulous attention to details. Every embroidery, every stitch, and every fabric choice is a testament to my commitment to perfection. I personally oversee the design process, ensuring that each garment not only looks exquisite but also feels comfortable and feels functional.

With each collection I hope to continue to push boundaries and redefine Indian fashion.
